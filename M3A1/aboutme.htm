Academic History and Goals:

In my Freshman year and Sophomore year of high school, I attended Jensen Beach High School and took some engineering classes, but I had a stronger interest in computer science. My Junior year I transferred to Clark Advanced Learning Center where I started taking classes more focused in computer science. I was part of Tech Team and was President of Computer Club. I was even nominated for the Pathfinder Award for computer science. I dual enrolled at IRSC at the time.

Then I went to Florida Polytechnic University for a year, pursuing a degree in cyber security, but the school just wasn't meant for me, so I transferred back to IRSC. Right now I am currently earning my AS degree in IT, and will decide to either continue my BS in computer science at IRSC or finish my degree at a different college. I am taking some applications for business classes as well as a web developmet class, a Java class, and a Linux class. Previous semesters I took classes focused on the C and C++ programming languages, but as of now I'm enjoying Java.

My goal is to finish my degree and have a job in the computer science field.
